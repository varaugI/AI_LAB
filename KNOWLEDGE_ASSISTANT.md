# Knowledge assistant

The knowledge assistant is one part of AI LAB, not the entire AI.

- Importing files creates immediate retrieval knowledge with citations.
- The local transformer or a fine-tuned pretrained model generates natural replies.
- Approved corrections become supervised fine-tuning examples.
- Deleting a document removes retrieval data but does not erase old trained weights.

See `README.md` and `ARCHITECTURE.md` for the current system.
